Lindsey Hannah:

Geospatial Professional

I am currently a Masters Student in the Urban Studies Progam at the University of Washington in Tacoma. Im interested in using geospatial data to make our cities a safer and more equitable place to live and to reduce human impact on the environment. I enjoy working with remotely sensed data and large datasets where I can dive deep.

Originally from Ohio, I found my way to the west coast as an Americorp volunteer with Habitat for Humanity. Before now, I've served in a lot of different roles, from building houses to overseeing major maintenance operations on nuclear powered vessels. Currently, I serve as the technical manager for a Japanese specialty building product manufacturer. I excel at active project management and working closely with customers, vendors, and staff to progress project goals. In my free time, you can find me hiking, throwing pottery on the wheel, backpacking, crabbing, baking, and petting all the dogs.
