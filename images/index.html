About me:

Geologist by training, mountain woman in practice, and certified map nerd

I am currently a Masters Student in the Urban Studies Progam at the University of Washington in Tacoma. Im interested in using geospatial data to make our cities a more equitable place to live with a focus on housing and resource availability.

Originally from Ohio, I found my way to the west coast as an Americorp volunteer with Habitat for Humanity. Before now, I've served in a lot of different roles, from building houses to overseeing major maintenance operations on nuclear powered aircraft carriers. Currently, I serve as the technical manager for a Japanese specialty building product manufacturer. In my free time, you can find me hiking, fishing, backpacking, baking, and petting all the dogs.
